Performing Your Best
When It Counts.

Mental performance coaching isn't about positive thinking. It's about building real skills that let you access your best — exactly when the pressure is highest.

This is what we work toward together.

  • Performing at your best in the moment — not just in practice
  • Walking into pressure situations with genuine confidence
  • Being excited to compete — not anxious, not dreading it
  • Consistency you can rely on, meet after meet, game after game
  • Building on success instead of waiting for the next collapse
Who I Am

Michigan Gymnast.
All-American.
National Champion.

I walked on at the University of Michigan and earned my spot. I went on to become an All-American, compete in the biggest moments, and be part of a team that won multiple Big Ten titles and a national championship.

At Michigan I was able to unlock high-level mental training which allowed me to compete with Olympic-level athletes on my events.

Road to Success

Training alongside elite athletes at Michigan made one thing clear: success is a complicated recipe. Talent, health, timing all need to align. But the ones who reached their ceiling weren't always the most gifted — they were the ones who trained their mind to optimise these ingredients.
